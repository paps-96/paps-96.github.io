Business impacts of COVID-19 data

The visualisations in this webpage are based on responses from the new voluntary fortnightly business survey, which captures businesses responses on how their turnover, workforce prices, trade and business resilience have been affected in the two week reference period. These data relate to the period 6 April 2020 to 19 April 2020. The survey was sent to around 17,600 UK businesses, and results presented in this release are based on a limited number of responses, around 34.7% (6,114) of all businesses surveyed who responded.
